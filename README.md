## Key Findings

The analysis identified several operational and financial risks:

- Negative cash position at the end of the reporting period
- High supplier concentration risk
- Significant growth of inventory balances
- Slow-moving inventory items
- Increased working capital tied up in inventory
- Accounts receivable concentration among several customers

---

## Recommendations

- Improve inventory turnover through better stock management
- Reduce supplier concentration risk by diversifying suppliers
- Strengthen receivables collection procedures
- Improve liquidity management and cash flow planning
- Implement regular KPI monitoring and analytical reporting
- Continue using data-driven decision-making practices
